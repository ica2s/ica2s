Advances in Artificial Intelligence for Society (ICA2S)

The International Conference on Advances in Artificial Intelligence for Society (ICA2S) brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss cutting-edge AI research and its societal impact.

Founded: 2025 · Annual International Conference

Call for Proposals (Ongoing)

ICA2S is organized as an annual international conference series. We welcome proposals from academic institutions, research organizations, and professional bodies interested in hosting future editions of ICA2S.

Call for Program Committee Members (Ongoing)

Active professors, research scientists, and senior researchers with terminal degrees are encouraged to serve as Program Committee members for current and future editions of ICA2S.
