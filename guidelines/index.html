Author Guidelines for Paper Submission via CMT

  1. Create a CMT Account
    • Visit the CMT website and register if you do not have an account.
  2. Paper Submission Process
    • Log in and search for the "ICA2S 2025" conference or click here
    • Select "Create a New Submission."
    • Enter the paper title, abstract, and author details (ensure all co-authors are listed correctly).
    • Upload the manuscript in PDF format (follow the conference template).
  3. Formatting Requirements
    • Use the official conference template (provided on the conference website).
    • Ensure the paper adheres to the page limit (including references).
  4. Keywords & Topics
    • Choose relevant topics and keywords to help in the review assignment.
  5. Plagiarism Policy
    • Papers must be original and free from plagiarism (checked via plagiarism detection tools).
  6. Finalizing Submission
    • Review all entered details and uploaded files before final submission.
    • Submissions cannot be edited after the deadline.
