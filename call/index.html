Call for Papers

The International Conference on Advances in Artificial Intelligence for Society (ICA2S 2025) aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss the latest advancements in AI and its impact on society.

Organized by Gulf Medical University Ajman, UAE, and the Indian Institute of Information Technology Bhopal, India, ICA2S 2025 will provide a platform to exchange ideas, foster collaborations, and explore the ethical, social, and technological dimensions of AI.

Topics of Interest

  • Technological Innovations in AI
  • AI in Healthcare: Transforming Patient Outcomes
  • Ethics, Privacy, and Security in AI Applications
  • AI-Driven Management and Decision Support
  • Cloud Computing and AI: Synergies for Scalability
  • AI for Society: Sustainability and Equity
  • Future Horizons in AI

Special Tracks

Track 1: Transforming Healthcare with Generative Artificial Intelligence Models: From Prediction to Precision (Call for Paper)


Track 2: Next-Generation Healthcare Intelligence: Deep Learning and Federated Approaches on HPC systems (Call for Paper)

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research work through the official conference submission portal. Submissions will be evaluated on originality, significance, technical quality, and clarity. Accepted papers must be presented at the conference by at least one author.

Submission Link: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICA2S2025

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: June 30, 2025 September 30, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: October 15, 2025
  • Final Camera-Ready Submission: October 30, 2025
