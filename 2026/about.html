About ICA2S 2026

Exploring AI's Impact on Society and Future

Join leading researchers, industry experts, and policymakers in exploring how artificial intelligence can solve real-world challenges and create positive social impact.

The International Conference on Advances in Artificial Intelligence for Society (ICA2S 2026) aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss the latest advancements in AI and its impact on society.

The International Conference on AI for Society (ICA2S) brings together brilliant minds from academia, industry, and government to explore how artificial intelligence can address pressing societal challenges. From healthcare to education, environmental sustainability to social equity, we examine AI's transformative potential.

Our conference features keynote speeches from world-renowned AI researchers, technical paper presentations, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities. Whether you're a researcher, practitioner, student, or policymaker, ICA2S offers valuable insights into the future of AI and society.
